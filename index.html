Hidden Risks

What Buyers Usually Don't See

A fresh coat of paint hides a lot. Most property problems aren't visible during a walkthrough — they show up after you've already paid.

Structural Risk

Hidden Structural Issues

Foundation cracks, compromised load-bearing walls, and low-grade construction materials are rarely visible to the naked eye — but they're expensive to fix.

Legal Risk

Title & Ownership Gaps

Missing chain deeds, unresolved encumbrances, and incomplete municipal approvals can make a property legally difficult or impossible to resell.

Moisture Risk

Water Damage & Seepage

Leaking pipes and failed waterproofing hide behind tiles and plaster. Left unchecked, they cause mold, structural decay, and repair bills that can run into lakhs.

Financial Risk

Unexpected Repair Costs

Faulty wiring, corroded plumbing, and failing systems often surface within the first few months of possession — when you're already committed and out of options.
